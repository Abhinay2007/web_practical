KJ College of Engineering and Management Research

About Us

Established in 2009 under the prestigious KJ's Educational Institutes (KJEI) umbrella, KJ College of Engineering and Management Research (KJCOEMR) has positioned itself as a premier destination for transformative technical education in India. Strategically located in Pune—India’s education and IT hub—KJCOEMR blends academic rigor, industry immersion, and research innovation to produce globally competent engineers. Affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University, approved by AICTE, recognized by the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Maharashtra, and accredited by NAAC, the college consistently benchmarks itself against the best in the world. We take pride in being a student-centered institution that champions academic brilliance, character development, and social consciousness.
